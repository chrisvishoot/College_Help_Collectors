Please refer to the Equivalency Guide Manual for important information regarding abbreviations and definitions
of terms.

Some Washington community college course prefixes
end with an ampersand (“&”) symbol and some do not. To avoid
confusion between courses, pay careful attention to ampersand designations.
Some courses that look similar (e.g., ENGL& 101 and ENGL 101) are actually
different courses and may have very different UW equivalencies.
